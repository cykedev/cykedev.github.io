automatisch aliase fÃ¼r den ssh zugriff auf alle hosts in `~/.ssh/config` erzeugen:

{% highlight bash %}
for HOST in $(cat ~/.ssh/config | grep "^Host " | sed 's/^Host //' | tr -s ' ' '\n' | grep -v "*"); do which $HOST > /dev/null || alias $HOST="ssh $HOST"; done
{% endhighlight %}
